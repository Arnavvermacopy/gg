About

Arti Chopra, a CBSE National Awardee, is a dedicated Educationist with over 28 years of experience as a teacher, mentor, trainer, planner, skill developer, and a tireless worker for the cause of children. She is a firm exponent of amalgamating modern skills and teacher empowerment in education. As an alumnus of Lady Shri Ram College, Delhi University, with a Masters degree in Economics and Business Administration; she has been associated with leading institutions like Bal Bharti Public School; Sawai Man Singh Vidyalaya, Lawrence School, Lovedale and currently heads Amity International School, Sector 46, Gurugram. With over 15 years as the Principal, she has been the torchbearer in integrating technology with education and has also been renowned, for displaying excellence in problem-solving, instructional design, strategy & academic planning. Honored by Microsoft, British Council & a number of leading universities and functionalities for tirelessly amalgamating technology in the learning process, curriculum development and for bringing about excellence in academics; as a CBSE Resource Person too, she inspires and trains educators for the all-round development of school children and works for the institutional development.
